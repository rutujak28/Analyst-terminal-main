🚨 The Analyst's Terminal

A dynamic market monitoring dashboard that simulates how analysts observe fast-moving financial markets.

The system brings multiple market signals together into a single interactive workspace where users can quickly understand what is happening across the market.

💡 Project Idea

• 📈 Financial markets generate thousands of signals every second
• ⚡ Prices change, sectors move, and unusual patterns appear rapidly
• 🧠 Analysts need a system that can observe multiple signals at once

The Analyst's Terminal acts like a financial control room where market information is continuously monitored and displayed.

🧠 Core Concept – Crit %

• ⚠ Crit % (Criticality Percentage) represents the overall stress level of the market
• 🔄 It updates dynamically based on market activity
• 📊 It helps analysts quickly understand how stable or unstable the market is

Crit % increases when:

• 📉 sudden price movements occur
• 📊 trading volume spikes
• 🌐 multiple sectors show unusual activity

Crit % decreases when:

• 📊 market activity stabilizes
• 📉 volatility reduces

🚀 Features
📡 Moving Market Ticker

• 🔄 A scrolling ticker at the top displaying market values
• ⚡ Continuously updates with simulated live data
• 👀 Helps analysts quickly scan major movements

📊 Market Indices

Tracks major market benchmarks such as:

• 🇮🇳 NIFTY 50
• 🇮🇳 SENSEX
• 🇮🇳 BANK NIFTY

• 📈 Shows the overall direction of the market

📈 Live Instruments

Displays individual stocks with:

• 💰 current price
• 📉 percentage change
• 📊 trading volume
• 📈 trend indicators

Helps identify which stocks are gaining or losing momentum.

🌐 Sector Pulse

Shows performance of major sectors including:

• ⚡ Energy
• 💻 IT
• 🏦 Banking
• 💊 Pharma
• 📡 Telecom

Sector activity often explains why Crit % goes up or down.

🔔 Anomaly Whispers

Highlights unusual market behavior such as:

• 📉 sudden price drops
• 📊 abnormal trading volumes
• ⚡ unexpected sector movement

Acts like an early warning system for analysts.

📊 Today's Movers

Displays the highest gainers and losers of the day.

• 📈 top gaining stocks
• 📉 top losing stocks

Helps analysts quickly focus on high-activity stocks.
